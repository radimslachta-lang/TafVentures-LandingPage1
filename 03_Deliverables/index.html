The Platform

Where Medicine Meets
Venture Capital

Tafelberg Ventures is the innovation and commercialization arm of Fakultní nemocnice Olomouc — one of the Czech Republic's leading teaching hospitals. We bridge the gap between world-class biomedical research and scalable healthcare businesses.

◈

For Startups & Innovators

Access clinical environments, anonymized patient data, medical expertise, and a validation pathway that investors trust. Turn your MedTech concept into a fundable, deployable solution.

◈

For Investors & Funds

Access a curated pipeline of hospital-validated health innovations. Benefit from early co-investment opportunities in de-risked, evidence-backed MedTech and digital health ventures.

◈

For Researchers

Transform discoveries into real-world impact. We provide a structured commercialization pathway, industry partnerships, and capital connections to bring research to market.

◈

For Healthcare Business

Co-create solutions directly within a leading clinical institution. Validate technologies, run pilots, and develop strategic partnerships with Central Europe's top medical center.
